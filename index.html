About Doseluru

Doseluru was born from a simple passion - the love for the perfect dosa. We realized that finding a truly great dosa spot was often a matter of luck or local knowledge.

Our mission is to be India's most comprehensive and trustworthy dosa discovery platform. We personally visit, taste, and rate every restaurant using our detailed 8-parameter system.

From the legendary heritage spots serving dosas for generations to the modern fusion kitchens pushing boundaries, we cover it all. Because every dosa lover deserves to find their perfect crispy, golden dosa.
